The main house
The architecture

Conceived from the land itself.

Designed by Eddie Roschi. Rammed-earth walls, wood, stone and clay sourced from the region. The architecture respects local Alentejo tradition while feeling modern and globally aware.

The estate sits low within the landscape and blends naturally into its surroundings. Pine forests, open fields, and fresh Atlantic air shape the atmosphere.

As a result, guests slow down, reconnect, and experience Portugal at a calmer pace — the way the region was always meant to feel.

The land

Melides, the quiet Alentejo coast.

Pristine beaches, pine forests, rice fields, open skies. The Atlantic to the west, cork forests to the east, the slowest part of Portugal in between.

Around the estate: horse riding along the beach, boutique wineries, seaside dining, coastal walks. The estate itself remains entirely private — no neighbouring houses, no traffic noise.

Booking conditions

What you'd ask before booking.

How does the booking process work? +

Send us your dates, group size and the kind of week you have in mind. Darina personally answers within two working days with three options at different price points (different inclusions / season / day count). You pick. We confirm with a deposit.

How do I pay for my stay? +

50% on confirmation (or 25% if booking more than twelve months out), final balance twelve weeks before arrival. Bank transfer or card. Invoice issued for both halves.

Will I be asked for a security deposit? +

Yes — refundable, returned within seven days of departure assuming no damages. Amount depends on group size and rental type. Stated clearly in your offer.

Check-in and check-out times? +

Check-in 4:00 PM, check-out 10:00 AM. Earlier check-in or later check-out negotiable depending on the calendar — just ask.

What additional services can be arranged? +

Chef for breakfast and dinner (organic, local, Alentejo-style). Yoga teacher on the deck under the olive trees. Wellness practitioners — massage, breathwork, sound baths. Excursions — wineries, horse riding, beach access, Comporta day trips.

All optional, all booked through us at vetted local rates.

Is the estate suitable for retreats? +

Yes — the estate was designed with retreats in mind. Six bedrooms across two wings give the host privacy from guests. Outdoor yoga deck. Chef-equipped kitchen. We handle transfer logistics from Lisbon airport. Most retreats book nine to fourteen months in advance.

How far ahead should I book? +

Private weeks: 3–6 months for shoulder season, 6–9 months for July–August peak. Retreats: 9–14 months — the group signup cycle runs longer. Weddings: 12+ months.
